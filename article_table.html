Set up a table the right way

  1. Place the plate in the center.
  2. Lay a folded napkin to the left of the plate
  3. Set your forks atop the napkin.
  4. Set the knives to the right facing inwards towards the plate.
  5. Following the knives place the spoons on the right.
  6. Set the glass or glasses on the right above the knives.
