Shake Hands The Right Way

  1. Give eye contact!!! For goodness sake give good eye contact, act like you care.
  2. Deliver a good, firm handshake. Not too strong but not floppy like a dead fish.
  3. Say something! If you're meeting this person for the first time say "Nice to meet you" or if you're departing say "It's been a pleasure". Simple things like this can make or break your exchange
